Pizza

Description

Homemade Pizza isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor — try this homemade pizza recipe today!

Feel free to substitute ricotta cheese for the cottage cheese for a more traditional cheese filling. You can also add layers of veggies (such as spinach and mushrooms) when layering your pizza.

Ingredients:

  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce

  2. Step 2 - Toss
